About Kenya Geospatial Project Monitoring System

Promoting transparency and accountability in development projects across Kenya

Our Mission

The Kenya Geospatial Project Monitoring System is a government initiative designed to enhance transparency, accountability, and citizen participation in monitoring development projects across the country.

By providing a platform for citizens to track and report on projects in their communities, we aim to:

  • Improve project implementation and outcomes
  • Reduce corruption and mismanagement of funds
  • Enhance citizen engagement in development processes
  • Ensure public funds are used effectively for public benefit

How It Works

Explore Projects

Browse ongoing and completed development projects across all 47 counties on an interactive map.

Monitor Progress

Track the implementation status, timelines, and budget utilization of various projects.

Report Issues

Citizens can report progress, problems, or provide suggestions for improvement on any project.

Government Commitment

This platform is part of the Kenyan government's commitment to the Constitution of Kenya (2010), which emphasizes transparency, accountability, and public participation in governance.

We believe that informed and engaged citizens are essential for effective development and democratic governance.
